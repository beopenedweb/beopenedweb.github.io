Our Vision

To enable our students to become a part of, rather than apart from the world of sound.



Our Mission

To teach children with hearing loss to listen & speak and thereby integrate them with necessary self-confidence and positive image into the society

Key Objectives

  1. Educating and socially integrating Children with hearing impairment.

  2. Providing a stimulating environment by which effective learning and teaching can take place

  3. Providing appropriate resources to enhance the delivery of curriculum

  4. Encouraging children to set high expectations of their performance and strive to attain them.

  5. Assisting children to unleash their full potential through positive reinforcement.

  6. Improve communication between deaf and normal communities to remove social segregation.







Taking part in implementing United Nations Sustainable Goals for 2030

We envision a world of peace, prosperity and opportunities for all. Our goals are aligned with the United Nations Sustainable goals – a blueprint for achieving equilibrium progress in all walks of life- especially for the downtrodden and physically challenged.


Fostering Holistic Solution for the hearing impaired for generations to come

At Ephphatha, we try to discover every method available to positively nurture hearing impaired children , while recognizing the ultimate goal of integrating them into society is the natural road to traverse while carefully considering the current issues of the time.

Bringing the digital edge to training

Every day, technological advancement breaks down challenges and anonymously fills human voids. At Ephphatha, we embrace this power of change that innovations spur by constantly updating ourselves with new tools and technologies – providing our students with every advantage possible.

Internet Information and Communication Tools (ICT) training

Hearing impaired students are often at a disadvantage in finding reliable sources of information that deal with personal and practical living skills. At Ephphatha, we empower these children by helping them gain knowledge to traverse the digital world to their advantage while also carefully ensuring that they avoid the pitfalls of the Internet with great responsibility and care.

Digital Technologies and Devices

From hearing aids, smart boards, mobile apps, PC applications, FM loops and more, Children gain new avenues to communicate like never before via technology. Our training module enables children to seamlessly adapt to disruptive technologies that open infinite possibilities to improve their communication with spontaneity - changing their world.

Building Our Reach
