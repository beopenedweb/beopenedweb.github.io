Be the change

Be the change that you want to see – Mahatma Gandhi.

Disabled and left to the mercy of others, these hearing impaired children’s resolve to rise above their circumstance never waivers. Yet, living in a world of isolation, they become devoid of joy and happiness – sooner or later their spirits break.

Sanctuary of hope

True wealth is not acquired through earthly possessions, but by leading a fulfilling life. There is nothing more fulfilling than knowing one has made a palpable difference in the lives of other people.

At Ephphatha, we empower these children to fulfill their aspirations. Our commitment to excellence across the board, be it training modules, research or infrastructure is a culmination of our best efforts.

Yet, we could always do more.

We need your helping hands to reach out to them. It may not come as wrapped gifts; but as improved systems, assistive devices, better facilities, etc. Anything that would allow them to face the challenges of the real world is a permanent gift that cannot be wiped away or taken back. The smiles and happiness will be permanent and their gratitude lasting.

Be Open, Be Heard!

Empower the children at Ephphatha, your contributions will change their lives. Giving them an opportunity to revel in a society where they have a voice.

A tiny stone can create a massive ripple when thrown into water at the right moment. This is your moment to make a splash by contributing to our cause.
