The challenges of integrating with the real world can be overwhelming for students with hearing impairment and at times what they learn in a classroom environment may not suffice when the students mix into society.

At Ephphatha, we help the student tackle problems head-on by equipping and training them with life- altering skills that make them employable. Literally, we propel them into the society fairly equipped and strong in character and boldness.



Self-Dependency

The challenges of interacting in society are aplenty for a person with hearing impairment. Imagine not being able to communicate with others in a shop or even a bus conductor coupled with the fact that reactions may not be the same. Harsh reactions can causes immense emotional damage to the hard-of-hearing causing them to withdraw, be hurt and ashamed.

At PROPEL, our training module prepares them to become mentally strong and tackle tough situations with reason and no emotional distress.
