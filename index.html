Ephphatha - “Be Opened”

A Catalyst of Hope for the Hearing Impaired

Bourne out of an innate desire to improve the lives of children with hearing impediments, Ephphatha has been in the service of the Hearing Impaired Children since 1983, enabling Children to articulate answers and respond to people. Our Aural-Oral methodology has enhanced their word power and vocabulary to make them more compatible in their schools, workplaces and other parts of the society.

"A person's vocabulary is knowledge of words already known by that person." - Caldwell and Leslie (2009)

If a person can provide a definition of a word then the person is able to attach a basic meaning to the word. The aural – oral method effectively helps children to use words on par with their normal peers.

Bridging the impairment gap through innovative solutions

Disabled Children are no longer limited by space; technology has now connected them globally. Knowledge and knowledge sharing has been has become cheaper, faster and at the touch of a button. Being left out means a total disruption in connecting with the society.

The ability to articulate and to understand is the underlying need of the hour and it is being achieved by evolving methodologies. Institutions need to be on par with their counterparts worldwide so that no child misses out on opportunities.

Continuous research, collaboration and upgrading of facilities and training of staff is required so that it can help these children to learn fast and gain the much needed confidence.
