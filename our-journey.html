Ephphatha - Our Journey

Inspiring Change, Overcoming Challenges

Taking the message of “Be Open” to impact the livelihood of hearing impaired children

Founded in 1988 as a faith-based social enterprise for the hearing and speech Impaired, Ephphatha’s 30 year walk along with the disabled children has enabled us to also learn, understand and find technologies, and methods to improve the lives of affected children.

Our unwavering commitment to impact the lives of children with hearing disabilities has helped us establish a well recognized and unique Institution following the Aural-Oral method of teaching in the country known as OSHI- Oral Higher Secondary School for the Hearing Impaired.

The school serves as a sanctuary of hope for many children affected with hearing disabilities – allowing us to enrich the lives of a cohort of young men and women.

Though OSHI proved a resounding success, the challenges for these children soon outgrew our accomplishments.

Recognizing the need to do more, we introduced two more programs - Shine - Speech and Hearing Interventions for Enabling and Propel - Program for Professional Education and Leadership.

These programs allowed us to find answers to underlying problems and create solutions that helped better their prospects for a brighter future.

Since our inception, we have charted countless success stories and made an impact with several of our students going on to colleges and leading a successful work life – becoming a part of society.

Through it all we have always found compassion through the endearing support we have received from well-wishers across the globe.
