Ephphatha is a specialist training and prep organization that aides the hearing impaired to adapt to mainstream living and to integrate themselves within a society by imparting skills and values that encourage sustainable living.

Opening a Spectrum of Possibilities

It is important that one understands the complexity of a process, be it a parent, donor or a career aspiring teacher. There are many schools for the deaf in the country following various methodologies. At Ephphatha, our Aural - Oral method encourages children to rely on words more than signs to communicate with others. This is a process, specially developed, difficult to impart, needs patience and effort on the part of teachers and students for effective implementation.
