Ephphatha - Our Future

A roadmap to Transformative Success

Fostering Holistic Solution for the hearing impaired for generations to come

At Ephphatha, we try to discover every method available to positively nurture hearing impaired children , while recognizing the ultimate goal of integrating them into society is the natural road to traverse while carefully considering the current issues of the time.

Bringing the digital edge to training

Hearing impaired students are often at a disadvantage in finding reliable sources of information that deal with personal and practical living skills. In the digital world reliable sources are available through the internet. By searching the Internet, they could obtain information without having to correspond through a telephone relay service, written notes, or interpreters.

At Ephphatha , we train and teach students to avoid the pitfalls of the Internet with great responsibility and care.

Building Our Reach

One of our prime objectives is to expand our services to children with hearing disabilities far and near. Through experience and relentless dedication we now have tools and knowledge to usher holistic transformation in the lives of the hearing impaired, yet we need your collective support to build on our progress and give us a bigger platform to pursue our cause.
